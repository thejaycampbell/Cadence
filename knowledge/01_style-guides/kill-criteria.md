# Kill Criteria

When to not publish, not ship, not send. Without this, mediocrity creeps in.

---

## Too Generic

- Could apply to any industry or person
- No specific mechanism or named observation
- Reader learns nothing they didn't already know

## Too Obvious

- Everyone already knows this
- No contrarian angle or tension
- No counterpoint worth making

## Redundant

- Already said this, no new layer
- Same framework, no progression
- Retreads ground without adding depth

## Doesn't Ladder Back

- Doesn't connect to a core claim in `03_projects/core-claims.md`
- Random content that doesn't reinforce positioning

## The Rule

When in doubt, kill it. The bar is high. Publishing mediocre content costs credibility.
Better to say nothing than to add noise.
