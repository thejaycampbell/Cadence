# ICP Evolution Log

Not just who your ICP is now — a running record of how it has changed and why.

Six months of this log is more useful than any current-state definition. The changes reveal which assumptions were wrong, what you've learned, and where you're actually finding signal.

Updated by `/weekly`. Add entries manually as your thinking shifts.
