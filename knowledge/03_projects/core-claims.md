# Core Claims

Your central beliefs and positioning statements. Written as claims, not categories.

Every piece of content, advice, or analysis should ladder back to these.
If it doesn't connect, reconsider whether it belongs.

Filled in by `/onboard`. Add new claims with `/add-claim`. Reviewed by `/weekly`.
